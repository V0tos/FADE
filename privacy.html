FADE – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Fade respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal data responsibly and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains: what information we collect; how we collect it; why we collect it; how it is used; which third parties receive it (including third-party AI services); how long we retain data (including face data); and how you can delete your data. By using the Fade mobile application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fade only collects information necessary to provide its features.

A. Images You Upload

We collect images that you voluntarily upload, including:

  • Face photos
  • Hair scans
  • Haircut scans
  • Product scans
  • Progress images
  • Images uploaded within AskFade for analysis or guidance

Images are used solely to generate AI-based analysis, scores, and recommendations within the app.

B. Onboarding Information

During onboarding, we collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • Gender
  • Date of birth
  • Where you found Fade (e.g. social media, referral, search)
  • Hair goals and preferences
  • Style interests

This information is used to: personalise recommendations; improve user experience; understand how users discover Fade. Fade does not use onboarding data for advertising profiling or sale.

C. Scan Results & Progress Data

We store: AI-generated scan results; scores and improvement estimates; progress tracking data over time. This allows users to track changes and trends.

D. AskFade Inputs

When using AskFade, we collect: text prompts you submit; images you upload for guidance. These inputs are used only to generate AI-based responses and are not used for advertising.

E. Usage & Technical Data

We may collect limited technical and usage data, such as:

  • Feature interactions
  • App performance and stability data
  • Error and crash logs

This data is used only to maintain and improve app functionality.

F. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you: enter it in the app; take or choose photos using the camera or photo library; submit prompts or images to AskFade; and use features that generate scan results. Data you provide is transmitted to our servers and, where described in this policy, to OpenAI for AI processing, using encrypted connections (HTTPS).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Analyse images using AI (via OpenAI as described below)
  • Generate haircut, styling, product, and improvement guidance
  • Respond to AskFade requests
  • Track progress over time
  • Improve Fade’s performance, accuracy, and reliability

We do not use your data for unrelated purposes.

3. What We Do NOT Do

Fade does not:

  • Sell personal data or images
  • Share images with advertisers
  • Use images for identity verification
  • Use images for biometric identification
  • Judge, label, or identify individuals

Images are analysed only to calculate visual proportions, patterns, and traits, not to determine identity or worth.

4. Image Consent & User Responsibility

Users must only upload images: of themselves, or of others where explicit consent has been obtained. Uploading images of others without consent, including for mocking, harassment, or abuse, is prohibited and may result in account suspension or termination.

5. AI Processing & Accuracy

All analysis, scores, and recommendations are generated by artificial intelligence systems.

Outputs are predictive and probabilistic in nature.

Results may be inaccurate or incomplete.

Outputs are for styling guidance only and are not guarantees.

Fade analyses facial proportions, structural characteristics, and hair traits to generate approximate recommendations. Fade does not determine identity, personal value, or objective attractiveness. Scores represent AI-generated estimations based on visual data.

6. Third-Party AI (OpenAI) — Who Receives Your Data

Fade uses a third-party AI service so we can analyse images and text. We identify that provider here and in the app before your data is sent for certain flows (for example, when you confirm before continuing from the side selfie step).

6.1 OpenAI, LLC

Provider: OpenAI, LLC (“OpenAI”), a United States–based company that provides AI models and APIs.

What may be sent to OpenAI: Photographs you upload (including face photos and other hair-related images), text you type into AskFade, and minimal technical information needed to complete an API request.

Why: To perform AI-based image analysis, generate scores and recommendations, answer AskFade questions, and produce haircut and styling guidance.

How we obtain your permission: By using features that require sending data to OpenAI after reading this policy; by accepting this Privacy Policy when you use Fade; and through in-app prompts that ask you to confirm before analysis proceeds where those prompts are shown. If you do not agree, do not use features that send data to OpenAI.

OpenAI’s privacy practices are described in its Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Processing and retention by OpenAI: Content sent to OpenAI is processed on OpenAI’s systems to generate a response. OpenAI may retain API content for a limited period for abuse monitoring, safety, security, and legal compliance, as explained in OpenAI’s Privacy Policy and API terms. We do not control OpenAI’s internal retention periods, but we use OpenAI only as necessary to provide Fade’s features.

Safeguards: We use contractual and technical measures aimed at ensuring that processing by OpenAI is consistent with applicable data protection requirements. We require our service providers to protect personal data appropriately.

6.2 Supabase (storage and backend)

Provider: Supabase Inc. provides database and file storage we use to host your account data and uploaded images.

What Supabase stores: Data we save on your behalf, including uploaded images (including face photos), scan results, and account information.

Why: To authenticate users, store your content, and sync the app across devices.

Supabase processes data as a service provider (processor) under our instructions. Supabase’s practices are described at: https://supabase.com/privacy

6.3 Other processors

We may use additional infrastructure or analytics providers only as needed to operate the app. They receive only what is necessary and are bound by appropriate agreements.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence (including the United States, where OpenAI and some of our providers are located). Where we transfer data internationally, we take steps to protect it in accordance with applicable law (e.g. standard contractual clauses or other approved mechanisms). By using Fade, you consent to such transfer where necessary to provide the service.

8. Data Storage & Security

Fade takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect user data. However, no system is completely secure, and using Fade involves inherent risk.

9. Data Retention (Including Face Data)

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

9.1 General

We retain user data while your account remains active and as needed to provide the service, unless you delete it sooner.

9.2 Face data — how long and why

How long: We retain facial photographs you upload for as long as your account remains active and you have not deleted them, because that period is necessary to provide scan history, progress comparisons, and personalised recommendations based on your past scans. We do not retain face data indefinitely without purpose: retention is limited to the time your account is active and the data is needed for these features, or until you delete it—whichever comes first.

Why this length: This duration matches the time you use Fade for ongoing styling and progress features. When you no longer need historical scans, you can delete your data (see below).

Deletion: When you use Settings → Delete My Data (or equivalent account deletion), we delete stored facial images and related personal data without undue delay as part of that process, subject to limited backups that are overwritten in the ordinary course.

9.3 Third-party retention of face-related data

OpenAI: When your images are sent to OpenAI for processing, OpenAI may temporarily process and retain content as described in section 6.1 and in OpenAI’s Privacy Policy. That retention is controlled by OpenAI’s policies, not an indefinite archive of your photos for Fade’s own use.

Supabase: Supabase stores your images as long as we retain them in our systems on your behalf. When we delete images from our product database and storage as part of Delete My Data, Supabase no longer retains them for Fade’s purposes except as may exist in short-lived technical backups in line with Supabase’s infrastructure practices.

10. Data Deletion & User Control

Users may permanently delete all stored data at any time via: Settings → Delete My Data.

This includes:

  • Uploaded images (including AskFade uploads)
  • Scan results and scores
  • Onboarding information (including gender and date of birth)
  • Progress history

Once deleted: data is permanently removed from active systems; it cannot be recovered through the app.

11. Facial Data & Biometric Information

Fade collects user-submitted facial photographs (front and side selfies) when users initiate scans or upload progress images.

These images are used exclusively to analyse facial proportions, symmetry, structural characteristics, and hair traits to generate personalised recommendations.

Fade does not perform facial recognition, identity verification, surveillance, or biometric identification. We do not create biometric templates for identifying individuals.

Facial images are transmitted securely to OpenAI for AI analysis and stored using Supabase as described in sections 6 and 9.

Facial data is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: access your data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion (also available via Settings → Delete My Data); object to certain processing; request data portability; withdraw consent where processing is based on consent; lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at fadeapporg@gmail.com.

13. Account Deletion

Deleting your data disables your account. Active subscriptions must still be cancelled separately through your app store.

14. Payments & Subscriptions

Fade offers paid subscriptions and premium features. All payments and subscription management are handled by third-party platforms, such as: Apple App Store; Google Play Store.

Fade does not collect or store: credit card numbers; debit card numbers; payment authentication details.

We may receive limited subscription-related information, such as: confirmation of an active subscription; subscription tier (weekly, monthly, yearly); subscription status (active or expired). This information is used only to manage access to premium features.

15. Third-Party Services

Fade relies on third-party services for: app distribution; payments and subscriptions; cloud hosting; and AI processing (including OpenAI). Those providers operate under their own privacy policies for their own business (for example, Apple, Google, OpenAI, Supabase). We are not responsible for practices outside what we control, but we describe here how we use those services with your data.

16. Children’s Privacy

Fade is not intended for users under the age of 15. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 15. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 15, please contact us and we will delete it.

17. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date. We may notify you of material changes in the app or by email where appropriate. Continued use of Fade after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. If you do not agree, you must stop using the app.

18. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact: fadeapporg@gmail.com
