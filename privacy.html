FADE – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Fade respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal data responsibly and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains: what information we collect; how we collect it; why we collect it; how it is used; which third parties receive it (including third-party AI services); how long we retain data (including face data); and how you can delete your data. By using the Fade mobile application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fade only collects information necessary to provide its features.

A. Images You Upload

  • Face photos
  • Hair scans
  • Haircut scans
  • Product scans
  • Progress images
  • Images uploaded within AskFade

Images are used solely to generate AI-based analysis, scores, and recommendations.

B. Onboarding Information

  • Gender
  • Date of birth
  • Referral source
  • Hair goals and preferences
  • Style interests

C. Scan Results & Progress Data

We store scan results, scores, and progress tracking data.

D. AskFade Inputs

  • Text prompts
  • Uploaded images

E. Usage & Technical Data

  • App performance data
  • Error logs

F. How We Collect Information

We collect data when you enter information, upload images, use the camera, or use features. Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Analyse images using AI
  • Generate recommendations
  • Respond to AskFade
  • Track progress
  • Improve performance

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • Sell data
  • Share with advertisers
  • Use for identity recognition
  • Create biometric identifiers

4. Image Consent & Responsibility

You must only upload images of yourself or with consent.

5. AI Processing

All outputs are generated by AI and may be inaccurate.

6. Third-Party AI (OpenAI)

  • Provider: OpenAI, LLC
  • Data sent: Images and prompts
  • Purpose: AI analysis and recommendations
  • Retention: Temporary processing for safety and compliance
  • Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

7. Storage (Supabase)

  • Provider: Supabase Inc.
  • Purpose: Database and storage
  • Data stored: Images, results, account data
  • Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

8. Data Retention

Data is stored while your account is active and only as needed for features like progress tracking. You can delete data at any time.

9. Data Deletion

Delete your data via: Settings → Delete My Data.

10. Facial Data

We analyse facial proportions only. We do not identify individuals or perform biometric tracking.

11. Security

We use encryption and secure infrastructure.

12. International Transfers

Data may be processed outside your country.

13. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data.

14. Payments

Handled by Apple or Google.

15. Children's Privacy

Not intended for users under 13.

16. Changes

We may update this policy.
