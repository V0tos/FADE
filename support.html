Last updated: 21 February 2026

If you need help with FADE, have questions about subscriptions, payments, technical issues, or account problems, please contact us directly.

Contact Support

Email us at:

fadeapporg@gmail.com

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 14)
  • Your iOS version
  • A clear description of the issue
  • Screenshots if relevant

Subscription & Billing Help

All payments and subscriptions are processed through Apple. If you need help with billing, refunds, or cancellation, you may also contact Apple Support directly through your App Store account settings.

Response Time

We aim to respond within 48 hours during business days.
